Why Fleet Pros?

01

Minimize Costs

Whether you are running a large company or simply starting out, costs can always be minimized. One of the first concerns in any business requiring a fleet is simply, how many vehicles are needed. At Fleet Pros, it is our mission to find you the perfect-sized fleet. Our dedicated team handles evaluation of the equipment and models necessary for your operation, ensuring you get the best price from the get go and further down the line.

  • Ideal-Sized Fleet - We will find the perfect fleet for you, alleviating unnecessary costs and upgrades, with minimal maintenance costs down the line.
  • Pricing Audit - Our experts are committed to keeping your finances in check, analyzing a slew of manufacturers to secure the best possible prices on your behalf.
  • Profitable Resale - Understanding the best time to sell your vehicles is an art. Our company constantly reviews optimal vehicle resale values to ensure you get the most bang for your buck, allowing you to reinvest in your business.
  • No Hidden Costs - Unexpected costs and upcharges are bad for business. What you see is what you get. Period.

Save Time

When it comes to managing a business, time is always of the essence. At Fleet Pros we understand that carrying out day to day operations in a timely manner is of utmost importance. Firmly grasping this concept is what enables us to provide you with solutions tailored to help your business run smoothly. We eliminate daunting and time-consuming tasks, allowing you to focus on what matters most.

  • Eradicate Tedious Tasks - We’ll handle all necessary paperwork, from licensing and registration to recall and maintenance negotiations.
  • Quicker Vehicle Turnaround - From purchasing vehicles to selling them at the right time, we’ll keep our hand on the pulse, letting you know the best time for vehicle replacement. Vehicle preparation, transportation and replacement are a headache of the past!
  • Swift Decision Making - Our fleet systems and infrastructure, coupled with a determined team of specialists, allow for fast and reliable decisions
  • Rapid Maintenance - We understand managing a fleet comes with a massive maintenance overhaul. Utilizing our network of experts, we will ensure all necessary maintenance is carried out promptly and efficiently.

03

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

One of the most overlooked aspects of running a fleet is understanding the longevity of your vehicles. Luckily, our experienced and dedicated staff of experts is here to make sure everything runs smoothly. From maintenance to fuel management, and everything in between, Fleet Pros has you covered. Our fleet development managers will supervise all expenses associated with vehicle ownership, providing you with full cost breakdowns in our quarterly reviews.

  • Knowledgeable Purchasing - Nobody comprehends seasonality shifts better than our team, guaranteeing you the best-priced vehicles no matter the time of year.
  • Maintenance Oversight - Utilizing our network of shops and service centers located nationwide, we recommend the best approach to vehicle maintenance based on it’s year, mileage, and warranty.
  • Flexible Replacement - Customizable financing enables you to pick options that fit your business best. A variety of vehicle terms, mileage plans, and cash options are available at your disposal.
  • Quarterly Reviews - Our fleet development managers pride themselves in making your fleet work for you. Our quarterly reviews provide in-depth analysis in fleet expenditures and guidance towards future fleet planning.

04

Safety First

Safety is crucial when dealing in the automotive industry. At Fleet Pros, safety is a high priority and primary concern for our customers. Making sure the vehicles deployed in your fleet adhere to the industry standard is paramount to leading a flourishing business. Our safety clause ensures that vehicles are equipped with the best parts on the market, at-risk drivers are actively identified, and insurance claims are appropriately handled.

  • Active Monitoring - Proactive vehicle maintenance is the key to vehicle durability and business longevity. Our fleet development managers actively monitor and track maintenance needs, informing you when your fleet should be serviced.
  • Clean Records - Employees are an integral part of your fleet. Our fleet development managers are there to help you identify at-risk drivers to reduce potential accidents.
  • Driver Compliance - Making sure you are protected from a legal standpoint is essential. Our fleet development managers assist in the implementation and adherence of company car policies.
